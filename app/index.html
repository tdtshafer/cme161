Baseball: Earning Your First Letter

In this visualization, I visualized Lahman's dataset on how many all-star games certain baseball players appeared in during the early years of Major League Baseball. Two of the data filters focus on the first letter in a word: one for the team name, and one for the player name. You can find out more information about this data set here .

Drug Use in San Francisco

This visualization shows data released by the San Francisco Police Department on reported drug cases in different regions around the city. The data can be accessed for free here. As you can see in the visualization, while the overall reported drug cases is falling drastically, one drug's cases may actually be increasing.

FBS College Football Mascots

This visualization shows the teams that have a certain mascot in the Football Bowl Subdivision of NCAA Division-I Football programs. The data can be accessed for free here. This dynamically generating visualization is an interesting tool for comparison and conference affiliation of mascots and other team name elements.
